General information

Costa Rica is awesome. It is located near Mexico it is in Central America. It has tons of beaches filled with black sand because of volcanic eruptions. It has cool snakes like vipers.It also has tons of monkeys. Some howler monkeys throw poop at you and then laugh at you. The have the best food ever."Adios!"

There are all sorts of animals in Costa Rica. There are tree frogs and lots off monkeys and very poisonous centipedes and tree vipers.

Costa Rica has all sorts of plants. It has palm trees, cool flowers, and jungle trees.

The best food in Costa Rica is Casado. (Description: A plateful of black beans, rice, tortilla and meat or fish.) Casado is a lunch staple popular all over the country.
